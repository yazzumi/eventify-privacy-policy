Account Deletion Request

Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Eventify mobile app by following these steps:

1
Open the Eventify app on your device
2
Go to your Profile screen (tap your profile icon in the bottom right)
3
Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account" (in red)
4
Enter your password to confirm your identity
5
Tap "Delete Account" to permanently delete your account

What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your profile information will be permanently removed
  • All your events and event history will be deleted
  • You will be removed from all groups and conversations
  • This action cannot be undone
  • Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes
